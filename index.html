About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, battery intelligence, and cyber-physical system resilience. With over 7 years of experience, my work focuses on secure and intelligent battery ecosystems for electric mobility and microgrids, validated through advanced PHIL and HIL platforms.

Research Interests

  • Cybersecurity in Battery Management Systems
  • Bayesian Neural Networks for Battery State Estimation
  • AI-Driven Battery Diagnostics via EIS
  • Grid-Edge Control and PHIL Frameworks
  • Federated and Privacy-Preserving Learning
